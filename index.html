Rent Automation

Set recurring rent charges with amount, due date, and notes. Once bank accounts are linked via Stripe ACH, LintelAI automatically sends reminders, initiates collection, and tracks payment status.

Maintenance Automation

Legal Documentation

Upload leases and agreements. LintelAI parses documents, stores structured data, and generates legally compliant notices based on local regulations.

Smart Inbox
